Who is The Lankan Table?

Welcome to The Lankan Table we are a popular five star restaurant located in the beautiful city of Kandy, Sri Lanka.
We serve a wide range of delicious traditional Sri Lankan food and desserts, made with fresh local ingredients and lots of care.
This is our official website, where you can explore our menu, place your orders online, and get your favorite meals delivered straight to your home.
Enjoy the taste of Sri Lanka from the comfort of your home with The Lankan Table!
